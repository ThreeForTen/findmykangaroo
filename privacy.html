Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Find My Kangaroo ("we", "us", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Location data: When you use the "Find My Kangaroo" button, your browser may share your approximate geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) with the Service. This is only done with your explicit permission via your browser's location prompt.

Place searches: If you search for a place by name, that query is sent to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding service to convert it into coordinates. We do not store these queries.

No accounts or personal data: We do not require registration, collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location data is used solely to calculate the distance to the nearest known kangaroo location. It is processed entirely in your browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — for geocoding place name searches. Their privacy policy applies to those requests.
  • Google Fonts — for loading web fonts. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • GitHub Pages — for hosting. GitHub's privacy statement applies.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Find My Kangaroo does not use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, or any form of tracking. We do not use advertising or remarketing services.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any user data. All location calculations happen in your browser and are discarded when you close the page.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no personal data is collected, no special provisions are required.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Singapore, including the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) where applicable.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us via the contact information on findmykangaroo.com.
